all about moosha

moosha is a London-based dirty soda bar bringing bold, non-alcoholic drinks at markets, pop-ups and private events across London.

Inspired by the idea that drinks should be just as fun without the alcohol, we specialise in custom-crafted sodas made with BIG flavour and BIG energy - drinks, made your way...

We are passionate about curating an inclusive space, social, and anything but boring! We specialise in curating drinks that people remember.

We’re proudly independent and proud to be London based.
