all about moosha

moosha is a London-based dirty soda bar bringing bold, non-alcoholic drinks to events, pop-ups and markets across the city.

Inspired by the idea that drinks should be just as fun without the alcohol, we specialise in custom-crafted sodas made with flavour, fizz and personality. Think classic favourites, playful twists and drinks built exactly the way you like them.

From private celebrations and brand events to community pop-ups, Moosha is all about good energy, great service and creating moments people remember. Every menu is thoughtfully curated, every drink is made to order, and every setup is designed to fit the vibe of the event.

We’re proudly independent, always evolving, and passionate about making inclusive, alcohol-free drinks feel exciting, social and anything but boring.
